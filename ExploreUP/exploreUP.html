Cuisine

Uttar Pradesh boasts a rich and diverse culinary heritage that reflects its cultural and historical depth. The food varies across regions, with distinct styles in the north/central plains and the Awadh region (especially Lucknow). The cuisine is broadly divided into two categories: vegetarian fare, which dominates most parts of the state, and the royal Mughlai/ Awadhi cuisine, known for its richness and intricate flavors.
